Briós

Description

Brioches are excellent for breakfast because of their slightly sweet flavour. But, making them fresh is the best experience one can have, being a sensation for all senses. Note that making them a day before eating them is not ideal. Still, they maintain taste. You can find the original recipe here.

Ingredients

  • 500 g fine flour
  • 70 g melted butter or margarine
  • 100 g powdered sugar
  • 25 g fresh yeast or (powder yeast 7 g)
  • 250 ml lukewarm milk
  • Two egg yolks
  • One egg for lubrication
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Turn on the oven with upper-lower heat 190 degrees.
  2. Add flour and sugar to a large bowl. Then mix it.
  3. Mix the yeast with a bit of warm milk and flour-sugar mixture.
  4. After a few minutes, the yeast mixture should rise. Add it to the bowl with the rest of the milk, butter, and egg yolks.
  5. The dough must be kneaded until a homogeneous mass is formed and the dough does not stick to the bowl.
  6. Afterwards, take the dough and let it rise for 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Now divide the dough into equal-sized pieces. Roll out these pieces and twist them into a tower.
  8. Let the pieces rise for another 15 to 20 minutes.
  9. Apply the egg on the dough pieces and put everything into the oven for 20 to 25 minutes.
